Why Heal Thy Soul?

I cant handle the toxicity from the content people share on instagram, facebook and other social media platforms. This made me think about establishing a user friendly platform where like minded people could share their emotions, laugh togethor, party togethor, cry togethor and fear togethor. Why do you need to know each other for sharing what holds you within?
